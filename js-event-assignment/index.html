Course Registration

BCOM (Bachelor of Commerce): This is a 3-year undergraduate program that covers areas like accounting, finance, business management, and marketing. It prepares students for careers in the business and corporate sectors.

BIT (Bachelor of Information Technology): A 4-year program that teaches the fundamentals of IT systems, programming, database management, cybersecurity, and network administration. It is perfect for those aiming for roles in IT development or system administration.

BMLS (Bachelor of Medical Laboratory Science): This program focuses on the laboratory sciences essential for diagnosing and treating various diseases. Graduates can work in healthcare laboratories or as clinical scientists.

BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration): A 3-year undergraduate degree that provides a solid foundation in business concepts such as economics, human resources, operations management, and organizational behavior. It prepares students for leadership roles in various industries.

BSCCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science): This is a 4-year program that focuses on computer science theories, algorithms, software development, data analysis, and AI. Graduates are equipped to pursue careers as software developers, data scientists, or systems analysts.
